One Pot Rice and Greens

Description

There are some parts of life—brushing teeth, taking a shower, drinking water—that are not showy but wholly essential, often performed without much thought or gratitude. I consider making a weekly pot of rice and greens one of these life skills.

This habit caught on at the start of the pandemic, when I needed both starchy comfort and an antidote to starchy comfort. Even better that it was a dish I could make with staples I’d purchased the last time I went to the market, however long ago that was.

Ingredients

  • Rice, or any other grain cooked with water
  • Dark leafy greens, like mature spinach or kale

Steps

  1. Cook rice as you normally would
  2. When rice is done cooking and resting, pile the greens on top of the rice and put the lid back on.
  3. Uncover after five minutes and season greens with salt and then fluff them into the rice.
